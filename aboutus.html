Company Overview

Prison break INC is a non-profit organization that was set up on May 21, 2021 to meet the educational, social and career needs of individuals with criminal records. Most times it is always believed that a person who has once been in prison will always be a liabilty to the society but this is not true. records have show shown that a prisoner or a person with criminal records can still be successful with proper guidance and determination. This is what motivated a group of individuals to come together to set up this organization. We plan to reach out to a lot of prisoners who are determined not to allow their past determine their future.

Mission

Dedicated to increasing the positive impacts of prisoners and ex-convict in the society

Vision

To be the World Known organization for helping ex convicts get a second chance.

Values

~Respect for all people
~Striving to be the best
~Promoting empowerment and diginity
