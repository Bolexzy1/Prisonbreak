Did You Know ?

Nelson Mandela

He was a south african patriot who was sentenced to life imprisonment for trying to overthrow the current government. he spent 27 years of his life confined to a small cell and he was only allowed to meet with visitors for 30 minutes every six months. Despite all this, he was not broken and was further motivated to achieve his goals and aspirations. On Febuary 11, 1990, Mandela was released from prison. In 1993, he was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize. On may 10, 1994, Mandela was sworn in as the first black president of South-Africa
